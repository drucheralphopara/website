Introduction

Overview.

I am a dedicated physician and public health leader with a comprehensive background spanning clinical practice, program management, and strategic leadership. With a trifecta of qualifications—MBBS, MSc, and MBA—I bridge the gap between medical science and sustainable health business models.

Currently serving as Chief Health Officer, I lead initiatives focused on maternal and child health, HIV/AIDS prevention, and health systems strengthening across Nigeria and the globe.
