About Me

Dr. Uche Ralph-Opara is an experienced Medical Doctor and Public Health Specialist with about two decades of experience in leading evidence-based public health interventions across the globe. With a strong background in managing programs funded by USAID, Global Fund, and the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, Dr. Ralph-Opara has led initiatives in HIV/AIDS, Tuberculosis, RMNCH/Family Planning, and Nutrition.

Currently serving as Physician at Project HOPE, Dr. Ralph-Opara oversees the organization's global health initiatives, providing strategic direction and technical expertise. Previously, she held roles as Deputy Chief Health Officer, Deputy Regional Director for Africa and Team Lead/Associate Director for maternal and child nutrition projects. Dr. Ralph-Opara is renowned for her exceptional leadership skills, program management expertise, and commitment to driving positive health outcomes. She is a 2026 Global Fellow of the Gates Foundation Funded Women Lift Health Leadership Program.
